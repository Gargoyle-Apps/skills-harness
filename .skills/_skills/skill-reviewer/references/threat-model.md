# Threat-model background

> Load only when documenting why a check exists. Not cited in routine review findings — the operational rubric is `security-checks.md`.

These are governance and threat-taxonomy sources that inform the rubric. Useful framing for the *why* behind each check.

- [OWASP GenAI Top 10](https://genai.owasp.org/llm-top-10/) — taxonomy for LLM-specific risks. LLM01 Prompt Injection, LLM02 Insecure Output Handling, LLM05 Supply Chain, LLM06 Sensitive Information Disclosure, and LLM08 Excessive Agency align directly with skill-reviewer checks.
- [MITRE ATLAS](https://atlas.mitre.org/) — adversary technique catalog for AI systems.
- [NIST AI RMF / GenAI Profile](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework) — governance framing for AI risk management.
- [CISA AI guidance](https://www.cisa.gov/ai) — government framing for enterprise AI deployment and security.
